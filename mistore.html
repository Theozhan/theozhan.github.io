MI® Home

A team analysis project for Xiaomi Home

CASE REVIEW

With the development of online shopping, consumers have a higher requirement of the quality of physical retail stores. Online stores have many comparative advantages comparing with physical retail stores. This case study aim to describe the existing problems and the information system in MI® Store by using Microsoft Excel, Access, and Adobe Illustration.

CASE TIMELINE

2018 March

BRAND

MI® Home | Consumer Electronics

About Xiaomi®

Xiaomi Corporation is a Chinese electronics company founded in April 2010 and headquartered in Beijing. Xiaomi makes and invests in smartphones, mobile apps, laptops, bags, trimmers, earphones, MI Television, shoes, fitness bands, and many other products.

ABOUT MI® Home

MI® Home are Xiaomi's franchise stores for exclusive Mi smartphones and ecosystem products. By uniting Mi Fans and Young Entrepreneurs with business thereby establishing a strong bond, the Mi Store program aims at providing entrepreneurship opportunities to the youth who will then function as brand evangelists in their respective markets.
